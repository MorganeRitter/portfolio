Survival game developed with Unity3D

PROJECT FOR THE 3D VIDEO GAMES COURSE

This video game has been realised by a three-persons team with the help of the Unity 3D game engine.

The goal of this game is to survive by using one’s environment and even by protecting oneself from it sometimes. The player can gather berries to eat, harvest wood, collect stones and water.

Weather features can also affect the player, as for example temperature (hot or cold).

he character progresses in a random generated environment, generated with a procedural method from Perlin noise. The environment’s height is generated thanks to this noise.
