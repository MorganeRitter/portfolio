Perception of dimensions, distances and speeds within virtual reality systems for HMD and screen.

MASTER SECOND YEAR PROJECT

This project aimed to evaluate the influence of different criteria on the perception of dimensions and speeds in virtual environments. This project has been realised with students of the engineer school of Télécom-Physique of the University of Strasbourg.

These criteria have been chosen from different research articles on the question. Here is a non-exhaustive list of the evaluated criteria:

  • Occlusion
  • Eye-to-eye distance
  • Scale hints
  • Interaction

These criteria have been evaluated by creating different scenarios which have been tested on a large sample of users who knew or did not know about virtual reality.
